Something Weird Is Happening...

Okay, so something strange is going on with my Easyship account.

I've been getting email notifications about shipments being created, but I didn't create them. At first, I thought it was just delayed notifications from my test shipment. But there have been like 15 shipments in the past week.

I checked my Easyship dashboard and there are dozens of shipments to addresses I don't recognize. Mumbai, São Paulo, Tokyo, Moscow. I don't even have customers yet! My website isn't even live!

My credit card bill is... not good. $3,847 in shipping charges. For shipments I didn't create.

Maybe it's a glitch? I've emailed Easyship support. They said they're investigating "suspicious activity" on my account. What does that even mean?

I'm starting to get worried. This was supposed to be my big break, not my big mistake.

- Michåel Scøtt, Concerned Entrepreneur
