Starting My Ecommerce Journey

Hello world! My name is Michåel Scøtt (yes, with the cool Scandinavian letters), and I'm about to embark on the greatest journey of my entrepreneurial career.

For years, I've dreamed of running my own ecommerce empire. I've read all the books - "The 4-Hour Workweek", "Rich Dad Poor Dad", even "The Art of War" (because business is war, right?). Now it's time to put theory into practice.

My business idea? I'm going to sell artisanal snow globes. But not just any snow globes - these will be LIMITED EDITION PREMIUM snow globes featuring famous business leaders. Think Warren Buffett in a snow globe. Bill Gates in a snow globe. Oprah in a snow globe. Genius, right?

The only challenge? Shipping. That's where I discovered Easyship. They have this amazing API that lets me integrate shipping directly into my website. I'm basically a tech entrepreneur now.

And speaking of being a tech entrepreneur - I just set up this GitHub repository for blogging my entire project! Pretty impressive organizational skills, huh? You can check out the source code and see how a real developer does it: github.com/easyship-autobot/scott-globes

Stay tuned as I document my journey from corporate drone to self-made millionaire!

- Michåel Scøtt, CEO & Founder of Scøtt Globes Enterprises
